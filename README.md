# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using SQL.

Database Tables
- customers
- products
- orders

Business Questions Answered

-What are all the customers in the database?
-What products are available in the coffee shop?
-What orders have been placed in the system?
-Which customers ordered which products and in what quantity?
-What is the total revenue generated by the coffee shop?
-How much revenue does each product generate?
-How many units of each product have been sold?
-Which product is the most popular based on quantity sold?
-How much money has each customer spent in the coffee shop?
-Who is the highest spending customer?
-How many orders has each customer placed?
-How many orders come from each city?
-Which cities generate the most revenue for the coffee shop?
-What is the average quantity ordered for each product?
-Which products have sold more than two units?
-How many orders are placed on each date?
-How much revenue is generated on each date?
-Which customers have never placed an order?
-What is the most expensive product on the menu?
-What is the cheapest product on the menu?

Tools Used
- MySQL
- SQL
